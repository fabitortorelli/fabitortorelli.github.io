ABOUT ME

Journey of Learning: Embracing Challenges and Growth

I am a person who loves to learn and I embrace my mistakes because it is through them that we truly learn.

During my time preparing for university entrance exams, I always struggled with mathematics. I remember when I had the chance to study at a private school and encountered complex numbers for the first time in math class; I was completely lost. However, over the course of four years, despite many mistakes, I managed to learn high school mathematics. This persistence led me to decide to study mathematics at university, and it was the best decision I ever made.

Today, I have the opportunity to work with data, and it is not easy. Every day brings new lessons and new technologies, and it is very easy to get lost along the way.

That's why I decided to create this portfolio, to have a sense of where I started and where I am going. It's a way to follow a timeline of knowledge. Along this journey, many obstacles have been encountered, and many more will be found, but we must not give up when one of them blocks our path. After all, it is by making mistakes, finding shortcuts, or ways to break through the obstacles that we learn.
